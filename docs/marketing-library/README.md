# CG Dynamics Marketing Library

Last updated: 2026-07-01
Status: Foundation

## Purpose

The Marketing Library is the verified knowledge base that powers the AI Workforce.

It exists so CG Dynamics can produce strategy, reports, campaigns and creative direction from trusted marketing knowledge instead of generic AI output.

## Core philosophy

AI is not the source of truth.

AI is the tool used to retrieve, connect, reason and apply the source of truth.

The library must focus on human behaviour, marketing fundamentals, evidence, local context and internal campaign learnings.

## Source hierarchy

### Tier 1 - Primary and foundational sources

Highest priority.

Examples:

- Classic advertising and copywriting books.
- Behavioural psychology books and research.
- Peer reviewed research.
- Official platform documentation.
- Official statistics and reputable market reports.
- Direct client interviews and approved client information.
- Actual internal campaign performance data.

### Tier 2 - Trusted professional sources

Use with judgement.

Examples:

- Established marketing practitioners with clear track records.
- Reputable industry publications.
- Recognised SEO and paid media research providers.
- Case studies with transparent data.

### Tier 3 - Internal learning

Useful but must be labelled clearly.

Examples:

- Staff observations.
- Account manager notes.
- Campaign reviews.
- Repeated client feedback.
- Sales or enquiry patterns noticed internally.

### Tier 4 - Low trust or excluded sources

Avoid as source material unless being used as an example of what not to do.

Examples:

- Generic AI written SEO blogs.
- Unsourced marketing claims.
- Engagement bait threads.
- Trend posts with no evidence.
- AI generated tips that cite no human source or data.

## Skill Card requirements

Each Skill Card must include:

- Title.
- Category.
- Principle.
- Source type.
- Source reference.
- Source confidence.
- Summary.
- Why it matters.
- How to apply it.
- Examples.
- Mistakes to avoid.
- Relevant industries.
- Relevant agents.
- Status.
- Last reviewed.

## Confidence levels

### High

Supported by primary sources, research, official documentation or repeated internal performance data.

### Medium

Supported by trusted professional sources, strong case studies or multiple internal observations.

### Low

Useful idea, but still needs verification or more performance data.

### Opinion

Clearly labelled client, staff or internal opinion. It can be useful, but must not be presented as proven fact.

## Client knowledge boundary

The Marketing Library should stay industry and principle focused.

Client specific knowledge belongs in active client records or client specific Skill Cards tied to the active client system.

Master library documents should not mention individual clients as permanent examples.

## First build target

Start with the structure before volume.

Recommended first batch:

- 10 copywriting foundation cards.
- 10 buyer psychology cards.
- 10 brand positioning cards.
- 10 SEO foundation cards.
- 10 paid ads foundation cards.
- 10 design and visual hierarchy cards.
- 10 South African market intelligence cards.

Quality matters more than quantity.

## Non negotiable rule

The Marketing Library must make CG Dynamics smarter, not noisier.

Do not add content just because it sounds useful. Add content because it is sourced, actionable and can improve decision making.
