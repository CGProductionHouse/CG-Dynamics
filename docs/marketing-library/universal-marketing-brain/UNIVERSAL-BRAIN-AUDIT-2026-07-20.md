Last updated: 2026-07-20

This audit prevents duplicate work and exposes where the system is genuinely weak.

## Overall conclusion

The Universal Marketing Brain has strong beginnings in copy, classic advertising and persuasion, but it is not yet balanced.

Strongest domains:
1. Human copy and language
2. Offer and commercial thinking
3. Consumer psychology source material
4. Creativity and cultural observation

Weakest domains:
1. Market and customer understanding
2. Positioning and differentiation
3. Visual communication and art direction
4. Campaign planning and channel roles
5. Ethics, legality and brand safety

## Build order confirmed

Proceed from Domain 1 downward.

No later domain should be marked implementation-ready until it meets the completion standard in the Universal Marketing Brain Blueprint.
