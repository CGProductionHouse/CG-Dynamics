# Skill Card — Separate Observation From Interpretation

Domain: Market and customer understanding
Status: Active

## Principle

Record what happened before explaining what it means.

## Observation record

Capture time, place, task, sequence, tools, interruptions, workaround, people involved, visible action and exact words.

## Interpretation record

Store the proposed meaning separately with author, supporting observations, alternatives, confidence and verification question.

Example:

Observation: three customers checked the entrance sign and then asked a waiter where to order.

Interpretation: ordering instructions may be unclear.

Bad record: customers find the restaurant confusing.

## Method

1. Write a neutral event description.
2. Remove motive words that were not directly stated.
3. Generate at least one alternative explanation.
4. Link the interpretation to all supporting observations.
5. Verify through further observation, interview or measured behaviour.

## Misuse warnings

- Visible emotion is not a confirmed motive.
- One observer's interpretation is not market truth.
- Do not clean away contradictory behaviour.
- Do not convert an observation directly into content without commercial relevance.

## Evaluation

Pass only when another reviewer can distinguish raw evidence from the analyst's conclusion.
