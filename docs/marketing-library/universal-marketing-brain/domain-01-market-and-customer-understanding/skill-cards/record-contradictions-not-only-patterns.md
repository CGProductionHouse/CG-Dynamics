# Skill Card — Record Contradictions, Not Only Patterns

Domain: Market and customer understanding
Status: Active

## Principle

Contradictory evidence is information. Do not remove it merely because it weakens a clean narrative.

## Method

For every activated insight record:

- supporting evidence;
- conflicting evidence;
- context differences;
- possible segment differences;
- possible time or occasion differences;
- unresolved questions;
- effect on confidence.

## Interpretation rules

A contradiction may indicate:

- different customer groups;
- different triggers or occasions;
- different decision stages;
- a weak sample;
- inaccurate reporting;
- changing market conditions;
- a false initial insight.

## Misuse warnings

- Do not average away meaningful differences.
- Do not call an outlier irrelevant without a reason.
- Do not create a new segment from every disagreement.
- Do not retain a preferred conclusion when stronger conflicting evidence exists.

## Evaluation

Pass when a reviewer can see what would falsify the insight and which evidence currently limits it.
