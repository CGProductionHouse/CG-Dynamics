# Skill Card — Find the Current Alternative

Domain: Market and customer understanding
Status: Active

## Principle

The real competitor is whatever the customer would do if they did not choose the proposed solution.

## Alternatives to investigate

- another brand;
- existing supplier;
- cheaper substitute;
- manual workaround;
- asking a friend or employee;
- delaying;
- doing nothing;
- avoiding the situation;
- accepting the problem.

## Method

Ask about the last real decision:

- What were you doing before this?
- What would you have done if this option did not exist?
- Why was the old approach still acceptable?
- What finally made it insufficient?
- What would make you return to it?

## Required output

Record alternative, context, perceived advantages, frustrations, switching costs, habit strength and evidence.

## Strategic use

Positioning and offers should compare against the actual alternative, not only named competitors. A new system may compete more with spreadsheets and habit than with another software company.

## Misuse warnings

- Do not manufacture a weak competitor for an easy comparison.
- Do not assume doing nothing is irrational.
- Do not ignore benefits of the current workaround.
- Do not call every alternative a pain point.

## Evaluation

The card passes when the system can explain why the customer has not already changed.
