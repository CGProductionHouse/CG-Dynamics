## Important limitations

- These cards are based on the source note and general knowledge of the book's position in advertising history.
- The full book has not been re-read for this extraction.
- Page numbers and chapter references have not been added. Manual verification against the source text is required.
- Do not use these cards as final cited research until verified.
- These cards are principle-focused and industry-neutral. They do not reference specific clients.
- These cards do not justify modern platform algorithm tactics. Hopkins wrote about print and mail order. The principles apply broadly, but the mechanics do not transfer directly to Meta, Google or TikTok advertising.
