# YouTube Platform Expert

Last verified: 2026-07-20
Review interval: 30 days
Status: Active foundation

## Platform role

YouTube combines search, recommendation, subscription, long-form viewing, Shorts and durable video libraries. It can serve discovery, education, proof, entertainment, product consideration and long-term brand memory.

## Core Skill Cards

### Match viewer promise to video delivery

Title, thumbnail and opening establish a promise. The video must deliver that promise without deceptive packaging.

### Design the viewing journey

Structure around:

1. reason to watch;
2. necessary context;
3. progression, proof or demonstration;
4. payoff;
5. relevant next action or next video.

Remove introductions that delay value without building necessary context.

### Separate Search, Browse, Suggested and Shorts intent

Do not apply one packaging rule to every surface. Search-led content should answer explicit intent clearly. Browse and Suggested require compelling relevance and satisfaction. Shorts require immediate mobile comprehension and may play a different campaign role.

### Use thumbnails as communication

A thumbnail should communicate subject, tension, result or recognisable moment at small size. Avoid clutter, unreadable text and false emotional expressions.

### Build series and pathways

Use playlists, end screens, cards, descriptions and consistent topic architecture to help a viewer continue. One successful video is not a content system.

### Diagnose satisfaction, not only clicks

Review impressions, click-through context, watch time, audience retention, returning viewers, traffic sources, comments, subscriptions, downstream actions and conversion. High clicks with rapid abandonment may indicate broken promise.

### Preserve rights and disclosure

Verify music, footage, releases, sponsorship disclosure, reused-content implications, child-directed content requirements and advertising policies.

## Official source register

Use YouTube Help, YouTube Creator Academy, YouTube Analytics documentation, recommendation-system explanations, advertising policies and technical specifications. Product mechanics expire after 30 days; durable storytelling principles remain in the Universal Brain.

## Evaluation

Pass only when audience intent, discovery surface, packaging promise, retention structure, rights, next-viewer path and meaningful metric are explicit.
