# TikTok Platform Expert

Last verified: 2026-07-20
Review interval: 30 days
Status: Active foundation

## Platform role

TikTok is a discovery, entertainment, search, culture, creator and demonstration platform. Strong work feels native to the viewing environment while remaining recognisably true to the brand.

## Core Skill Cards

### Earn the next second

Open with a visually and verbally clear reason to continue: action, tension, result, question, recognisable situation or useful promise. The payoff must match the opening.

### Build around a human behaviour

Use real people, processes, products, places and reactions. Do not imitate slang, trends or creator mannerisms the brand has not earned.

### Show rather than announce

Prefer demonstration, transformation, process, comparison, participation and evidence over a presenter merely claiming that something is good.

### Design for sound-on and sound-off comprehension

Use clear speech, captions and visual sequencing. Rights and commercial-music eligibility must be verified for the account and intended use.

### Treat search as audience intent

Use language people genuinely search for in spoken words, on-screen text, captions and metadata where appropriate. Do not stuff irrelevant phrases.

### Separate trend participation from strategy

A trend is usable only when it advances the objective, fits the brand, remains understandable after the trend fades and can be executed in time. Otherwise decline it.

### Diagnose retention, not only views

Review opening hold, watch time, completion, rewatch, shares, saves, profile actions, search discovery, comments and conversion. Interpret metrics relative to video length, audience and objective.

## Anti-patterns

- deceptive hooks;
- recycled watermarked content;
- trend imitation without entitlement;
- overproduced advertising that hides the human point;
- underproduced material with no information or entertainment value;
- using a viral outlier as a permanent rule;
- unsafe challenges or unverified claims.

## Official source register

Use current TikTok Business Help Centre, Creative Center, Creator Academy, Community Guidelines, recommendation explanations, advertising policies and technical specifications. Mechanics and specifications expire after 30 days.

## Evaluation

Pass only when the first-second promise, human truth, brand entitlement, demonstration value, search intent, rights, safety and business metric are documented.
