# Google Business Profile Expert

Last verified: 2026-07-20
Review interval: 30 days
Status: Active foundation

## Platform role

Google Business Profile supports local discovery, credibility and action across Google Search and Maps. Accuracy and operational maintenance are more important than posting volume.

## Core Skill Cards

### Treat business information as conversion infrastructure

Verify business name, primary category, additional categories, address or service area, phone, website, hours, special hours, attributes, services, menu and booking paths.

Do not add keywords to the business name unless they are part of the real-world name and permitted by current policy.

### Match profile structure to real operations

Use storefront, hybrid or service-area configuration according to how the business actually serves customers. Do not create fake locations, virtual offices or duplicate profiles.

### Use photos as current proof

Maintain accurate exterior, entrance, interior, product, service, team and accessibility imagery. Images should help a customer recognise the location and know what to expect.

### Manage reviews as research and service

Respond honestly and specifically without exposing private information. Classify recurring praise, objections, operational failures and customer language for the Client Brain.

Never buy, gate or fabricate reviews.

### Keep hours and temporary changes current

Update holidays, closures, relocations and exceptional operating hours before customers encounter them. A promotional post cannot compensate for incorrect core information.

### Measure local action

Track calls, directions, website actions, bookings, messages where available, search visibility and qualified enquiries. Interpret platform-reported metrics within their documented limits.

## Governance

- verify ownership and admin access;
- protect account security;
- follow representation and eligibility guidelines;
- record changes and suspensions;
- do not promise reinstatement outcomes;
- maintain location-specific truth for multi-site clients.

## Official source register

Use current Google Business Profile Help documentation for eligibility, representation, categories, hours, posts, photos, reviews, performance and policy. All product mechanics and eligibility claims expire after 30 days.

## Evaluation

Pass only when profile facts match the real business, local action paths work, reviews are handled ethically, current operating changes are reflected and the recommendation cites current Google documentation.
