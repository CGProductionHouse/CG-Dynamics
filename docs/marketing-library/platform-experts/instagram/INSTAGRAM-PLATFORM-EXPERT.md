# Instagram Platform Expert

Last verified: 2026-07-20
Review interval: 30 days
Status: Active foundation; current mechanics require recurring verification
Territory: Global unless a feature is account- or country-limited

## Platform role

Instagram is primarily a visual discovery, entertainment, relationship and identity platform. Its useful business roles include:

- earning discovery among non-followers;
- building brand memory through repeated distinctive creative;
- demonstrating products, experiences and processes;
- maintaining familiarity with existing audiences;
- creating conversation through comments, replies and direct messages;
- moving people toward profile visits, enquiries, bookings, shopping or external conversion paths.

It should not be treated as a single feed. Feed, Reels, Stories, Search, Explore, Profile and direct messages serve different user situations.

## Strategic surface map

### Feed

Role: relationship, proof, brand building, considered viewing and saved reference.

Use for:

- strong single images;
- carousels with real information or narrative sequence;
- proof, launches, case work and offers;
- material followers may want to save or share.

### Reels

Role: discovery, entertainment, demonstration and reach beyond followers.

Use when movement, sequence, personality, transformation, sound or a rapid visual explanation adds value.

Do not convert every static idea into video without a reason.

### Stories

Role: immediacy, familiarity, participation, reminders, behind-the-scenes proof and direct response among warmer audiences.

Use for time-sensitive updates, interactive stickers, event coverage, availability, informal proof and conversation prompts.

### Explore and recommendations

Role: non-follower discovery. Eligibility is separate from merely being allowed on the platform. Sensitive, misleading, low-quality or unoriginal material may be permitted but not widely recommended.

### Search and profile

Role: intent capture and credibility check. Names, bios, captions, location signals, profile structure and pinned content should help a person understand what the business is, where it operates, why it matters and what to do next.

## Current ranking model: safe operational interpretation

Instagram uses multiple ranking and recommendation systems rather than one universal algorithm. The expert must avoid claims such as “the algorithm always rewards X.”

Operationally, optimise for:

- relevance to a defined audience and situation;
- the likelihood of meaningful viewing, interaction, saving, sharing or continued use;
- originality and recommendation eligibility;
- clear format-to-intent fit;
- relationship signals for follower-facing surfaces;
- strong early comprehension without deceptive hooks.

Observed performance rules must remain experiments until repeated and reviewed.

## Core Skill Cards

### Choose the surface before the format

Trigger: a brief asks for “an Instagram post.”

Steps:

1. Define objective.
2. Define audience temperature and situation.
3. Select surface.
4. Select format within that surface.
5. Define primary action and metric.

Failure: choosing Reels, carousel or Story merely because it is fashionable.

### Make the first frame explain the promise

For Reels and carousels, the opening should quickly establish subject, tension, benefit, proof or curiosity. It may create an information gap but may not deceive.

Check:

- Can the correct audience understand why to continue?
- Is the first frame visually readable on a phone?
- Does the payoff match the opening?

### Build for shares and saves only when the idea deserves them

Shares and saves are outcomes of usefulness, relevance, identity or emotional value. Do not add generic “save this” language to weak material.

Use save-oriented structure for reference value, instructions, checklists, menus, schedules or considered inspiration.

Use share-oriented structure for recognisable truths, group relevance, useful alerts, humour, identity and experiences people want others to join.

### Preserve originality and material value

Do not repost unmodified work, use watermarked recycled content or imitate trends without brand relevance. When adapting existing footage, add meaningful original context, editing, commentary, proof or storytelling.

### Design captions to complete the communication

The visual should carry the central idea where possible. The caption may add context, proof, detail, story, terms, location and action.

Avoid:

- captions unrelated to the creative;
- keyword stuffing;
- empty motivational language;
- long copy that repeats the same sentence in several ways;
- invented customer claims.

### Treat Stories as a sequence

A Story sequence should have a deliberate beginning, progression and action. Avoid publishing ten disconnected frames because assets exist.

Possible sequence:

1. context or trigger;
2. proof or experience;
3. detail;
4. participation or action.

### Match measurement to purpose

Possible diagnostic measures:

- discovery: non-follower reach, qualified profile visits, watch behaviour;
- value: saves, shares, completion, replies;
- relationship: repeat viewers, replies, meaningful comments and direct conversations;
- conversion: link action, message, call, booking, sale or tracked lead;
- brand: repeated reach, direct search, qualitative recall and client-specific brand indicators.

No single metric proves business impact.

## Recommendation and safety checks

Before publishing, check:

- claim support;
- music, image and footage rights;
- consent for identifiable people;
- regulated goods or sensitive-category restrictions;
- recommendation eligibility;
- account status;
- accessibility, captions and readable text;
- location and pricing accuracy;
- competition or promotion terms;
- whether the execution is original enough to add value.

## South African operating considerations

- Do not assume every account has the same commerce, music or advertising features.
- Verify local availability and account eligibility before promising a feature.
- Mobile data sensitivity may affect long, heavy or unclear video performance; treat this as a client-specific hypothesis, not a universal rule.
- Local language, humour and cultural references require actual client and audience evidence.
- Location clarity matters for local businesses serving specific towns, suburbs or travel routes.

## Official source register

Primary sources to review and archive in the application:

- Instagram Help Centre: ranking, recommendations and account status
- Instagram Recommendation Guidelines
- Meta Transparency Centre: recommendation and ranking explanations
- Instagram for Creators: current best-practice guidance
- Meta Business Help Centre: formats, specifications, advertising and measurement
- Meta Community Standards and advertising standards

Source records must store exact URLs, access date, territory, affected surface and next review date. Search summaries or creator commentary may not replace these sources.

## Expiry rules

- ranking and recommendation claims expire after 30 days;
- format and technical specifications expire after 30 days;
- stable strategic surface roles review after 90 days;
- client performance observations review after each meaningful campaign cycle;
- retired features remain historical and are excluded from generation.

## Evaluation checklist

A recommendation passes only when:

- objective and audience situation are explicit;
- surface selection is justified;
- the idea remains strong without algorithm superstition;
- current eligibility and specifications are verified;
- primary metric matches the desired behaviour;
- the recommendation cites last-verified platform knowledge;
- uncertainty and account limitations are disclosed.
